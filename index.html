Welcome!

This interactive platform allows you to explore the behavior of ideal gases in various conditions. Ideal gases are substances exhibiting predictable relationships between pressure, volume, and temperature. Through our simulation, you can observe how these gases behave under different scenarios, gaining insights into their properties and applications in the real world. Dive into our simulation by navigating to Simulation and discover the behaviors of ideal gases.
